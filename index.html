Quick Tip — the mirror & slide trick

Drop the bill into the grid. Mirror it down a row, slide it right by one cell (that's × 0.1 — your 10%). Mirror that row again and add the two: you've got the 20% tip with grade-school arithmetic.

Try: 23.40, 112.75, 9.99

Why this works

Multiplying by 10 shifts every digit one column to the left. Multiplying by 0.1 shifts every digit one column to the right — that's exactly what 10% of a number looks like. To get 20%, mirror that 10% row and add the two; column-by-column addition gives the tip without a calculator. For 15%, take the halfway point between the 10% row and the 20% row.
